About Me


Hello there! In case I didn't introduce myself on the home page, my name is Warren Van Zuydam. Heavily introverted, and I love to play around on computers. I've dabbled in all kinds of random things, from oil painting, to coding apps for my Android device, to learning how to make websites from scratch, etc. My aim is to have my personal space... my home on the internet. This website is all my doing, coded from scratch. I stumbled upon a YouTube video, telling me why I should make my own website. And I agree with the whole idea. Social media and other web apps have really made the internet this stale, corporate, same-y space. Less people want to make their own personal websites. I feel that we need to keep the indie web alive and encourage eachother to make a website - a place on the internet we can call "home".
